Our Company

Dillon Gage is comprised of over a hundred employess in Addison, Texas, New Castle, Delaware, Ontario and Los Angeles, California. Last year, the Dillon Gage Companies had over 2 billion dollars in sales.

After a long career in the brokerage industry, Stephen W. Miller founded the first Dillon Gage Company in 1976. Through the years, he added additional companies and services that followed his initial mission to provide professional and service-oriented leadership that makes Dillon Gage the leader that it is today. These companies include:

  • Dillon Gage Incorporated - A company dealing in the futures markets.
  • Dillon Gage Securities - a full-service FINRA and SIPC member firm offering stocks, bonds mutual funds, annuities, etc.
  • Dillon Gage Private Wealth Management - A fee based registered investment advisor.
  • Dillon Gage Insurance Services - An independent insurance agency, offering home, auto, life, home, and long term care insurance.
  • International Depository Services of Canada Inc. - a secure and insured precious metals and certified coin storage facility near Toronto.
  • Delaware Diamond State Depository- A wholly-owned, independently-operated precious metals storage facility located in New Castle, Delaware.
  • Dillon Gage Digital Metals - a resource responsible for delivering advanced tools and technologies that enable dealers to be more successful in their businesses. The company offers cloud-based solutions for physical precious metals built upon the Digital Metals Platform.
  • FizTrade - which offers real time trading for bid and ask markets for gold, silver platinum and palladium.

These are truly niche companies with unique products and a service orientation second to none.
